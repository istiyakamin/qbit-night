## Textbooks

### Free Online Textbooks
- [Qiskit Textbook](https://github.com/Qiskit/textbook) - Comprehensive, free, and interactive
- [Quantum Computing: An Applied Approach](https://link.springer.com/book/10.1007/978-3-030-83274-2) - By Jack Hidary
- [Learn Quantum Computation using Qiskit](https://github.com/Qiskit/textbook)

### Classic Textbooks
- **Nielsen & Chuang** - "Quantum Computation and Quantum Information" (The bible of quantum computing)
- **Preskill's Lecture Notes** - Available free online from Caltech
- **Mermin** - "Quantum Computer Science: An Introduction"
