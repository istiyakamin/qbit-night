# Learning Roadmaps

Structured learning paths to guide your quantum computing journey.

## Available Roadmaps

### [Beginner Roadmap](beginner.md)
For those new to quantum computing. Covers foundational concepts and basic programming.

### [Intermediate Roadmap](intermediate.md)
For those with basic knowledge looking to deepen their understanding.

### [Advanced Roadmap](advanced.md)
For experienced learners ready to tackle complex algorithms and applications.

## How to Use These Roadmaps

1. **Assess your level**: Read the descriptions above to find your starting point
2. **Follow the structure**: Each roadmap has weekly topics and milestones
3. **Practice regularly**: Complete the suggested exercises and projects
4. **Track progress**: Use the checkboxes to mark completed topics
5. **Join discussions**: Collaborate with others at the same level

## Customizing Your Path

These roadmaps are guidelines, not strict rules. Feel free to:
- Skip topics you already know
- Spend more time on challenging areas
- Explore tangential topics that interest you
- Combine resources from different levels
