## How to Participate

1. **Read** the challenge description carefully
2. **Work** on the challenge (individually or in groups)
3. **Submit** your solution before the deadline
4. **Discuss** approaches and solutions with the community
5. **Learn** from others' solutions

## Challenge Levels

- **Beginner**: Foundational concepts, basic circuits
- **Intermediate**: Algorithms, protocols, multi-qubit systems
- **Advanced**: Research topics, optimization, real hardware
