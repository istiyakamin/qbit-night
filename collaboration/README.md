# Collaboration Hub

Welcome to the Quantum Q Bit Night collaboration space! This is where our community comes together to learn, share, and grow.

## Weekly Learning Challenges

Each week, we post a new learning challenge to help you practice and apply quantum computing concepts.

### Current Challenge
See [challenges/](challenges/) for the current and past challenges.

### How It Works
1. A new challenge is posted every week
2. Work on the challenge individually or in groups
3. Submit your solution by the deadline
4. Share your approach in the discussion
5. Learn from others' solutions

## Templates

We provide templates to help you get started:

- [Challenge Template](templates/challenge-template.md) - For creating new challenges
- [Project Proposal Template](templates/project-proposal.md) - For proposing new projects
- [Meeting Notes Template](templates/meeting-notes.md) - For documenting meetings

## Study Groups

Join a study group to learn with others at your level:

### Forming a Study Group
1. Post your interest in the discussions
2. Find others at a similar level
3. Set up regular meeting times
4. Use the meeting notes template

### Study Group Guidelines
- Meet regularly (weekly or bi-weekly)
- Take turns presenting topics
- Share resources and notes
- Support each other's learning

## Code of Conduct

We are committed to providing a welcoming and inclusive environment.

### Our Standards
- Be respectful and inclusive
- Accept constructive criticism gracefully
- Focus on what's best for the community
- Show empathy towards others

### Unacceptable Behavior
- Harassment of any kind
- Trolling or insulting comments
- Personal or political attacks
- Publishing others' private information

### Reporting Issues
If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, please report it to the maintainers.
